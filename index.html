Our Story

Founded in 1999 by former military intelligence officers and special forces operatives, Sentinel Elite was born from a vision to bring military-grade security expertise to the private sector. What started as a small team of five elite professionals has grown into a global security powerhouse with operations spanning 40+ countries.

Our founders recognized a critical gap in the security industry: the need for services that combine traditional protection with cutting-edge technology and intelligence-driven strategies. This philosophy remains at the core of everything we do.
